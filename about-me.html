Hi! My name is James Han.

I currently work on personal projects, such as this website, and study a conversational real estate leasing AI, independently from 2017 and with the help of AppFolio since January 2019. I spend my time at the intersection of ML and product, trying to integrate them seamlessly.

Previously, I researched and implemented equity trading strategies at Two Sigma Investments between 2012 and 2017 after completing my bachelor's degree in mathematics at the University of Waterloo.
