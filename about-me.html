TL;DR What's James Han's story?

    Relationships, especially between people, are significant enough where individuals seek something outside of themselves to provide them meaning in this world. Whether that be physical, emotional, mental, and/or spiritual bonds. Why that is, and how to satiate the need is what James seeks to know more about.

    James researched further into this phenomena at Binghamton University, focusing on neuroscience to try to understand the psychological and the biological mechanisms of why people are intrinsically drawn towards relationships, and qualify the value of every entity thereof. Thus far, he believes that each individual has been pragmatically and genetically coded to belong to and be a part of something greater than ourselves; whether that be for survival and/or purpose.

    After some time, James came to realize that individuals are drawn to the responsiveness of computers in a similar way that people are drawn to other people. Computers and software are created by people to meet the needs of people. By using computers as mediums, people are inevitably tailoring computers to become anthropomorphous and enabling people to fulfill their needs, the same of how another person would, if not better and automated. And the application will keep on expanding until computers become a part of every process in life.

    It's a take-and-take relationship with computers, with the consumers continuously taking. It's also a relationship where expectations are consistently met by the computer. With advances in general technology and artificial intelligence, these relationships might exponentially increase the one-sided relationship. Ironically however, behind every computer and software are individuals, or corporations, who create and meet the relational demands of others. So one can argue that the relationship might actually be a take-and-take that's flipped, where the people behind the computers are the ones who ultimately take from other people.

    It comes down to people-to-people relations, and James chooses to prioritize individuals rather than the idea of it and the mediums. With this in mind, James continues to learn about computers and their relative software to be aware and stay ahead of this new virtual reality and its ubiquitous impact on relationships today, to better serve individuals. Pride and greed sets a path of selfish ambitions without the genuine regard of individuals. James believes that this path will assuredly lead to emptiness and lack of purpose and fulfillment. James is praying that he will be humble and not be deceived.

    James Han: "I love to be around people and to encourage others in love and peace because that's all that matters in the end. One problem that I ran into and later realized that it affected every relationship I've observed, was misunderstandings and a lack of humbleness that facilitates open communication. Change starts from me, and I've resolved to develop a clear and consistent communication with those around me within the context of peace and the application of technology."
