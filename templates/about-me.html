[TL;DR] What's James Han's story?

    His dad has worked in IT so naturally he had a lot of electronic devices around the house growing up, including computers. When he was in elementary school, he and his brother would stay up until the lights turned off to play a local game of Starcraft against computers, or NPCs. There were days when Starcraft didn’t perform as it should or days when James and his brother couldn’t play together on LAN. It was instances like these that nurtured his critical thinking skills to solve real life problems; it was pretty serious then. He understood that there was always an answer to a problem, it was only a matter of skillful deduction and the level of relativity; from the easy (the Starcraft CD needed to be cleaned) to the hard (having to install TCP/IP protocol for the computer to communicate to an interconnected device so that he and his brother could play together on LAN). James's love for computers would go on into adulthood and by then he had encountered countless errors and problems that he resolved. But the growth of a computer-related career was not in his agenda at the time as it was something he enjoyed, rather he felt the need to pursue a career in helping others in a “face-to-face” manner.

    Relationships between people are significant compared to any other relationship between anything else in the world. During his time at Binghamton University, he researched further into this phenomena to try to understand the psychology and neuroscience of why people are intrinsically drawn to each other and how to optimally fulfill that interaction and the existence of both parties. This desire had led me to choose a career path in therapy to first-handedly serve and interact with others.

    It wasn’t until mid-2019, when he became baffled at the understanding of another reality that had its own set of laws and rules, and how ubiquitous it is on every sector of life imaginable. This reality of programming presents him with the space and ability to materialize his hopes and dreams in the form of an application or client that humans will interact with. He wants to utilize his knowledge in human behavior to service people and better existing and future relationships. Impacting each individual to categorically transform generations to come.

    James Han: I love to be around others and making everyone feel comfortable. One simple problem that I ran into, and later realized affected every relationship I've been in, was misunderstanding and miscommunication. I had resolved to find a cure and have narrowed my search into the subjects of the mind and simple math.
